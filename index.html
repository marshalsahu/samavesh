About Us

Samavesh or the Devanagari meaning of "Confluence of Ignited minds" is the Annual Techno-Management fest of Veer Surendra Sai University of Technolory,Burla. Ever since its inception Samavesh has lived oupto its name of being the largest technical gathering in the state. Samavesh witnesses huge gathering of technocrats, business moguls,industry experts, scientistss and students who expressed keen interest in technology.

Science Fiction or popularly known as Sci-fi is the central theme of this year's fest which is testimony to the fact that what may be ignored as a fiction may be tomorrow's biggest creation. To replicate its wonder is the very thought of this year's fest and would prove exhilarating yet challenging for our next generation.
